sandwich, in its basic form, slices of meat, cheese, or other food placed between two
slices of bread. Although this mode of consumption must be as old as meat and bread,
the name was adopted only in the 18th century for John Montagu, 4th earl of Sandwich.

Ingredients

  • Slice of breads.
  • cheese.
  • meat.
  • ham.
  • lettuce
  • tomatoes
  • Some other food.

Preparation

  1. take one slice of bread
  2. place lettuce
  3. place tomatoes
  4. place cheese
  5. place ham
  6. place meat
  7. place any other food that you want
  8. take another slice of bread, and close the sandwich
